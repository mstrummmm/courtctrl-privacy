Privacy Policy

CourtCTRL

CourtCTRL does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No user accounts or login
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No advertising
  • No third-party SDKs collecting data

Match data is stored locally on the device only during active use and is discarded when the match ends.

Bluetooth is used only to connect to external score buttons (e.g. Flic). No Bluetooth data is stored or transmitted to any server.
